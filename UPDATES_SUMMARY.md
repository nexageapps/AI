## Usage Guide

### For Daily Learning

1. Open a lesson notebook
2. Work through the content
3. Complete the practice problems (in exam prep section)
4. Fill in the progress tracker:
   - Check off completion status
   - Add completion date
   - Rate your understanding
   - Write notes and reflections
5. Review key takeaways before moving to next lesson

### For Exam Preparation

1. Identify relevant lessons for your course (use course mapping)
2. Review key takeaways for quick overview
3. Work through practice problems without looking at solutions
4. Check common mistakes to avoid them
5. Test yourself on essential concepts
6. Use README progress tracker to see overall completion

### For Spaced Repetition

1. First review: 1 week after completion
2. Second review: 1 month after completion
3. Third review: 3 months after completion
4. Update "Last Reviewed" date each time
5. Adjust understanding rating as you improve
